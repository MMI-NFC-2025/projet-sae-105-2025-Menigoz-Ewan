Movie

Creation (2009) by Jon Amiel

Synopsis

This biographical film recounts the turmoil of Charles Darwin as he works on "On the Origin of Species." It portrays his groundbreaking scientific discoveries and his religious convictions.

Cast

Paul Bettany (Charles Darwin)
Jennifer Connelly (Emma Darwin)

Link with Mary Anning

Mary Anning's fossil discoveries played a crucial role in shaping Darwin's theory on evolution and extinction of species.

Movie Excerpt

Scenes where Darwin confronts his scientific discoveries with his personal doubts, highlighting the conflict between science and religion in the 19th century.
