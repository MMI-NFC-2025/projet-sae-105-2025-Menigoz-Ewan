About

Our Project

This website is an educational project dedicated to raising awareness of the Matilda Effect and to recognizing Mary Anning, a pioneer in paleontology whose contributions were long minimized.

Created in 2025, this project aims to make visible the work of women scientists erased from history, and to inspire new generations to pursue their scientific passions without barriers, limits, or constraints.

We believe that acknowledging the past is essential to building a fairer future in the field of science.

Our Mission

Educate

Inform the public about the Matilda Effect and its consequences on the history of science

Honor

Pay tribute to Mary Anning and forgotten women scientists

Inspire

Encourage diversity and inclusion in scientific fields
