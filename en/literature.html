Women in Science in Literature

La Voleuse de vers

Natacha Henry

A novel that highlights the erasure of female scientists throughout history.

Faire entendre sa voix

Leslie Kern

Essay and manifesto about the place of women and their struggle to be heard.

L'effet Matilda

Nathalie Anton

The book directly cites the Matilda effect that erases the recognition of women.

Les oubliées des sciences

Hélène Merle-Beral

Lists and documents the forgotten women who shaped science.

Themes Addressed

Recognition of female scientists

These books restore the visibility of women whose names have been erased from the history of science.

The Matilda Effect

They shed light on the systemic denial of women's contributions in favor of male colleagues.

Inspiration for new generations

By highlighting the journeys of these women, these works encourage girls to pursue scientific careers.
